# Targa Energy

Targa Energy is a midstream natural gas and natural gas liquids (NGL) services company operating in North America. The company provides gathering, compression, treating, processing, and selling of natural gas; storing, fractionating, treating, transporting, and selling of NGLs.

**Website:** https://www.targa-energy.com

## About

Targa Energy operates as part of the broader Targa Resources enterprise, one of the largest independent midstream infrastructure companies in North America. The company focuses on the Permian Basin and other major natural gas production areas.

Targa Energy does not have a public developer API or developer program.
